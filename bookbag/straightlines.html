STRAIGHT LINES

Sometimes you just need to go somewhere.
Not necessarily anywhere in particular. Say it's nice outside, and you want to explore a bit. You need to take a walk. You don't want to get lost. Naturally, you'll want to go in as straight a line as possible, so that you don't get confused about all the turns you make.

Enter STRAIGHT LINES.

With STRAIGHT LINES, you can pick a spot anywhere on Earth, and with the power of OpenStreetMap, you can get a path that you can travel on the road by foot, as straight as possible for as long as possible. So now you can plan your straight-line trip without anxiety. More options to come.
