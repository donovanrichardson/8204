BABYTALK

Defending infant literacy is among the most important challenges facing our society.
BABYTALK supports the literacy of children across America by reducing the number of letters required for reading.

Right now, the English language has 26 letters, and while that's just fine for the big kids and for the adults, babies' brains and mouths just can't handle all of the letters. BABYTALK's technology lets you reduce the number of letters in text, so that you can make reading easy for your baby.

Try BABYTALK in the text box below to give your tyke a head-start on literacy.


Enter a value from 1 to 24 and then click babify! 1 gives minimal babification while 24 provides ultimate baby literacy.
